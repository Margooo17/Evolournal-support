How do I get started with Evolournal?
After downloading the app, you can start journaling right away. The app will guide you through the initial setup, including how to build your Identity system, how to use WishBoard & Evolution Benchmark, etc.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take your privacy very seriously. All journal data is stored on your device and can optionally sync to your iCloud account. We do not access or store your personal journal content.
How can I backup my journal?
The app supports automatic iCloud backup. You can also manually export your journal data in the settings.
What devices are supported?
Evolournal supports iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later.
